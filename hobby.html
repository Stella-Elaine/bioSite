About Sam

Sam is a dedicated and curious learner who pivoted into the tech industry after working in various support roles. Originally joining a call center, Sam quickly transitioned into mobile testing for a grocery delivery app. Now a front-end developer focusing on React Native, Sam enjoys building features that improve post-order experiences like receipts and complaints. Outside of work, Sam is returning to school to pursue a long-term goal of transitioning into back-end development and DevOps.
